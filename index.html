About

Assistant Professor, Department of Philosophy
Chonnam National University

Research interests: My research focuses on historical and philosophical issues that arise in different areas of biological and cognitive sciences.
- First, my works concentrate on the concepts of mechanism, explanation, and models, which have been central to the New Mechanism.
- Second, I am also interested in issues that arise in artificial intelligence, neuroscience, and cognitive science by investigating a variety of philosophical concepts: computation, learning, moral judgment, consciousness, evolution, etc..
- Third, in order to analyze those concepts within both scientific fields, I use a mathematical framework from the structural point of view with category theory.
